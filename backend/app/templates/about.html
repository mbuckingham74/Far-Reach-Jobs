About Far Reach Jobs

How We Started

Far Reach Jobs began with a simple frustration: searching for jobs in Alaska's remote communities meant visiting dozens of separate websites for individual cities, tribal organizations, boroughs, and small municipalities. There was no central place to see what opportunities existed in bush Alaska and other extremely rural areas. We built this tool to solve that problem.

Our Mission

Our goal is straightforward: help job seekers like us find opportunities in remote communities, and help employers in those areas reach more candidates. Rural organizations often struggle to attract talent simply because people don't know the jobs exist. We're working to bridge that gap.

Built Ethically

We believe job search tools should serve job seekers, not exploit them. Far Reach Jobs is built on these principles:

  • Respects robots.txt — We honor website crawling guidelines and scrape responsibly
  • Links directly to employers — Every job links back to the original posting agency, not through us
  • No data collection — We don't harvest your personal information or track your job searches
  • No ads — We don't sell advertising or sponsored job placements

Who This Is For

Whether you're looking to relocate to Alaska's bush communities, already live in a remote area and seeking local work, or an employer wanting to expand your candidate reach, Far Reach Jobs is here to help. We focus on opportunities that other job boards often miss — positions with tribal councils, rural school districts, remote health clinics, village corporations, and small-town governments.

Community Project

Far Reach Jobs is a community-focused project. If you're an employer in a remote area and want your jobs included, reach out to us — there's no cost to get listed. We're always looking to expand our coverage to help connect more rural communities with potential employees.

For Employers

Are you an employer in a remote community? Get your jobs in front of candidates who want to work in rural areas — completely free.
