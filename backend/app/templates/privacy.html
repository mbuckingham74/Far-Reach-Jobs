Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 27, 2025

The Short Version

Far Reach Jobs does not collect, store, or share any personal information. We don't use cookies for tracking, we don't require accounts to browse jobs, and we don't sell data because we don't have any to sell.

Information We Don't Collect

Unlike most websites, we have intentionally built Far Reach Jobs to operate without collecting user data:

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No tracking cookies or advertising identifiers
  • No browsing history or search queries
  • No location data
  • No device fingerprinting

Analytics

We use a self-hosted, privacy-focused analytics tool (Matomo) to understand basic site usage such as page views and general traffic patterns. This analytics is configured to respect your privacy: it does not use cookies, does not track you across sites, and anonymizes IP addresses. No personal data is collected or stored.

Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage solely to remember your dark mode preference. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

External Links

Job listings on Far Reach Jobs link directly to employers' websites. When you click on a job listing, you leave our site and are subject to that employer's privacy policy. We encourage you to review the privacy practices of any external sites you visit.

Employer Submissions

If you're an employer and submit a job or careers page through our For Employers form, we collect only the information you provide (organization name, job details, contact email). This information is used solely to process your submission and contact you if needed. We do not share employer contact information with third parties.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this page. Given our commitment to not collecting data, we don't anticipate significant changes.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us.
